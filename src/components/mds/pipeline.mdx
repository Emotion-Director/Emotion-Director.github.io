Pipeline of Emotion-Director. **MMP-Agent** (left) implicitly integrates textual and visual information through four steps to construct a high-quality, emotionally diverse dataset, named ETI. **MMP-Diffusion** (right) effectively achieves emotion-oriented text-to-image generation by simultaneously learning textural and visual prompts and modifying the original DPO loss function.
